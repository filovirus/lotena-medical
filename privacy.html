Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Lotena Medical Inc. ("we", "our", "us") develops clinical reference applications including CodeIQ, CodeScreen, and CodeCalc. This privacy policy explains how our apps handle your information.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to work entirely offline and do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require user accounts or login
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect health or medical data
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not use cookies

Data Storage

All data entered into our apps (such as search queries, patient age for screening recommendations, or diagnosis codes for claim building) is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server. No data is stored beyond your current session unless you explicitly save it (e.g., favorited codes), in which case it is stored only on your device.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. All clinical data (ICD-10 codes, screening guidelines, vaccine schedules) is bundled within the app and does not require network access.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are clinical reference tools intended for healthcare professionals. They are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
